# Snapshot+™ Report — 10-Page PDF Outline

This document outlines the structure for the paid Snapshot+™ PDF deliverable.

## Page 1 — Cover

- Wundy mascot variation
- Brand Snapshot+™ title
- Client name + company
- Brand Alignment Score™ prominent
- Report date
- Wunderbar Digital branding

## Page 2 — Executive Summary

- What Wundy analyzed
- The brand's current maturity stage
- Key positive themes
- High-impact gaps/opportunities
- CTA to schedule a Blueprint™ consultation

## Page 3 — Brand Alignment Score™ Deep Dive

- Full explanation of scoring
- Comparison to industry benchmarks
- Confidence score (derived from input depth)
- Weighted breakdown
- Bar/Gauge graphic

## Pages 4–8 — Pillar Deep Dives (1 page per pillar)

For each pillar (Positioning, Messaging, Visibility, Credibility, Conversion):

### Top Section:
- Pillar Score (0–20)
- Rating label (Excellent / Strong / Steady / Mixed / Needs Attention)
- One-sentence summary

### Middle Section:
- What Wundy saw in your business
- What this means for growth
- Opportunities unique to your brand

### Bottom Section:
- 2–3 custom recommendations

## Page 9 — Your Brand Opportunities Map

- Top 5 opportunities ranked by impact vs. effort
- Visual map (quadrant chart)
- AI-generated "Quick Wins" list
- "Momentum Opportunities" (mid-term)
- "Long-Term Strategic Growth Moves"

## Page 10 — What to Do Next

- Snapshot+™ summary
- Invitation to upgrade to Blueprint™ or Blueprint+™
- Client-ready next steps:
  - "Do-It-Yourself Action Path"
  - "Done-With-You"
  - "Done-For-You"
- Button: Schedule Your Strategy Session →
- Wunderbar Digital contact / branding

## Implementation Notes

- Use React-PDF or Puppeteer for generation
- All trademarks (™) must be included
- Brand colors: #021859 (navy), #07B0F2 (blue), #27CDF2 (aqua)
- Font: Helvetica Neue / Arial
- Professional, clean design matching Wunderbar Digital brand
